When I was a kid,

my dad had a great speaker system.

I would come home from school and plug in my ipod. Laid out on the rug, I’d turn out the lights and just listen to whatever I could get my hands on.

Minkebell is about that feeling. It’s a place for audiophiles to find new music, musicians to find inspiration, and newcomers to gain a greater appreciation.
